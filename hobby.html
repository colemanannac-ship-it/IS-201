Passion of Anna Coleman

My passion is running. I have been running for about 13 years. I competed in Cross Country for seven years, and Track and Field for four years.

My dad has been running for many years and has been my biggest inspiration. He has always encouraged me to keep on going, even when things got hard.

I have never trained or ran to be the fastest I could be. I run because of the peace and clarity it gives me.

Running makes me feel good in my mind and body. I do not need to do it to get a certain pace down, I just need to do it where I can love it.
